About

History and Milestone:Chevalier Charitable Society (CCS) is a Non-Profitable Organization run by the Congregation of the Missionaries of “The Sacred Heart” (MSC) providing help to the needy and enable them to sustain and lead a stable life in the society. The Missionaries of the Sacred Heart of Jesus (MSC) is a missionary congregation in the Catholic Church founded in 1854 by Father Jules Chevalier from France. This Congregation has a vision of a new world emerging with the message of God's love and care for all men and women and to evoke a response in every human heart. In June 1998, Fr Adrian Meaney founded the MSC Mission Office in Australia with the general aim to act as an advocate for the poor and to challenge society to adopt policies which incorporate social justice principles. Taking forward the legacy of its founder to serve with Love and care Chevalier Charitable Society is turning into a beam of hope for the needy in India. The Chevalier Charitable Society was established in India on 8th September 2004 as an independent mission and legally registered under the laws and jurisdiction of India on 16th of July 2005. Since 2005 CCS has been actively involved in serving people and the society to fulfill its mission & Vision which includes Clean water projects, Educational and Medical Assistance.

Our Services

Pure Drinking Water

➤Providing water tanks, and pumps, and drilling bore wells in villages, schools, and organizations where the people have water scarcity or no supply of clean drinkable water. We provide pure drinking water to those who have access only to contaminated water which is not suitable for human consumption.

Learn more

Education

➤Help the village schooling children who are orphaned or single-parented or financially incapable to pay their school fees and meet their educational material needs like bags, notebooks, writing materials, etc. We provide them with financial support to meet the school fees and to buy other educational articles.

Learn more

Medical Aid

Conduct and support awareness programs to enhance knowledge on the cause and Treatment of HIV/Aids and other diseases. We also support them with medical help and to buy medicines on the regular basis for poor sick people.

Learn more

Home for Aged

Caring for the aged and elderly people who do not have anyone to care for them and who cannot take care of themselves. We run a home for the aged centre to assist and care for the sickly and aged people.

Learn more
